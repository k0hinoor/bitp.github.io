ABHIVYAKTI’19

IEEE BIT PATNA Student Branch organized the largest TECHNICAL FEST of BIT Patna- ABHIVYAKTI’19. It provides the platform to showcase skills and grit with a chance to win real prizes. Sports and extracurricular activities play an essential part in our lives, so these events include technical and fun events.

Engaging in these activities revitalize student’s thoughts and alleviates all our pressure. These events and activities are the most exciting element in campus life as a student is under immense strain. These activities assist students to relax and gave them exposure to managing such events successfully in the future.
